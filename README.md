# 📊 Sales Dashboard | Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview
This interactive Sales Dashboard was built in **Tableau** to analyze sales performance across multiple business dimensions. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key sales metrics, identify top-performing products and countries, and track sales trends over time for better business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze sales by country.
- Compare product performance.
- Evaluate sales distribution by product size.
- Track sales trends over time.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Cards
Provides a quick overview of the most important business metrics, including:
- Total Sales
- Orders
- Profit (if available)
- Other key performance indicators

### 🌍 Sales by Country
Visualizes sales across different countries to identify:
- Top-performing markets
- Geographic sales distribution

### 📦 Sales by Product
Displays product-level sales performance, helping identify:
- Best-selling products
- Products with lower performance

### 📏 Sales by Size
Analyzes sales based on product size categories to understand customer purchasing patterns.

### 📅 Sales Over Time
Shows sales trends over time, making it easier to:
- Detect seasonality
- Monitor business growth
- Identify sales fluctuations
